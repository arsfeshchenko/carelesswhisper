# Parrrot 🦜

A macOS menu bar app that turns your voice into text — and types it for you.

## How it works

1. **Hold** your hotkey
2. **Speak** your mind (or mumble, it's fine)
3. **Release** — Parrrot transcribes your words via OpenAI Whisper
4. **Boom** — text gets pasted wherever your cursor is

Optional: enable **Auto-submit** and it'll also hit Enter after pasting. Like a parrot that not only repeats you but also sends the message before you can regret it.

## The bird lives in your menu bar

No dock icon. No window. Just a tiny bird up top, waiting to repeat everything you say.
